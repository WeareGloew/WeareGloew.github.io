Join the G.L.O.E.W sisterhood

Whether you’re a young woman wanting to join, or a professional wanting to refer, this page explains how to get started.

Who it’s for

Eligibility

G.L.O.E.W is designed for young women aged 18–25 who:

  • Have experienced or are at risk of unhealthy or abusive relationships, trauma or emotional harm.
  • Are ready (or curious) to explore healing, boundaries and self-worth in a gentle, supported space.
  • Can attend weekly sessions for the 12-week cohort where possible.
How to join

Enrolment & consent

Step 1

Enrolment form

This form collects basic details, contact information, support needs and any accessibility considerations. It can be completed by the young woman herself or with the help of a professional.

Complete enrolment form

Step 2

Consent form

Before joining the cohort, we ask each participant to complete a consent form, which explains what the programme involves, how we handle information and how we work together to keep the space safe.

Complete consent form

Referrals

Information for professionals

We welcome referrals from social workers, youth workers, support workers, housing teams, women’s services and other practitioners working with young women who might benefit from G.L.O.E.W.

We can provide:

  • Programme outline and risk assessments on request.
  • Attendance updates (with consent) and summary feedback.
  • Signposting and onward referrals for additional support where needed.

For referral conversations, please email us via the Contact page.
