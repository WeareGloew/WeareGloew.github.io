Join the G.L.O.E.W sisterhood

Whether you’re a young woman wanting to join, or a professional wanting to refer, this page explains how to get started.

Who it’s for

Eligibility

G.L.O.E.W is designed for young women aged 18–25 who:

  • Have experienced or are at risk of unhealthy or abusive relationships, trauma or emotional harm.
  • Are ready (or curious) to explore healing, boundaries and self-worth in a supported space.
  • Can attend weekly sessions for the 12-week cohort where possible.

Referrals

Information for professionals

We welcome referrals from social workers, youth workers, women’s services, housing teams and other practitioners.

  • Programme outline and risk assessments (on request)
  • Attendance updates (with consent)
  • Support with onward referrals where needed

For referral enquiries, please reach out via the Contact page or use our Professional Referral Form. Submit a Professional Referral

Privacy Notice: G.L.O.E.W handles all personal information with care and confidentiality. Any details shared through our enrolment or referral forms are used solely for the purpose of assessing suitability for the programme, supporting communication, and ensuring participant safety and wellbeing.

Information is stored securely and only accessed by authorised G.L.O.E.W staff. We do not share your data with any outside organisations unless required for safeguarding, risk management, or where explicit consent has been given.

A full Privacy & Data Protection Policy will be published once G.L.O.E.W is formally registered as a CIC and ICO-certified. If you have any questions about how your information is used, please contact us at weare.gloew@gmail.com.
