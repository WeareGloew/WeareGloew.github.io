The G.L.O.E.W 12-week journey

A carefully designed programme blending healing, confidence, relationships, safety, money skills and soft-life moments — so growth isn’t just talked about, it’s lived.

Overview

Three phases of the cohort

Phase 1

Weeks 1–4: Safety, stories & self-awareness.

Welcome day, icebreakers, personality tests, story sharing, temperature checks and reflective writing on life plans, trauma responses and identity.

Phase 2

Weeks 5–8: Confidence, body & voice.

Spoken word training, “Lay it Bare” therapeutic circles, movement workshops (like pole art for confidence), self-defence and safety awareness.

Phase 3

Weeks 9–12: Future, freedom & soft life.

Money mindset, financial literacy, education and training opportunities, celebration sessions, reflection, and an end-of-cohort treat.

Week by week

Sample cohort outline

Week 1

Welcome, story mapping & confidence check-in.

Icebreakers, personality tests, writing exercises on trauma responses and life goals. Pre-programme evaluation and group agreements.

Week 2

Money, independence & imposter syndrome.

Mentoring around financial literacy, practical skills, mindset work around worthiness and taking up space in education, work and money.

Week 3

Education, work & opportunity.

Support with CVs, training, college/university referrals, and pathways into courses or qualifications that fit the young woman’s goals.

Week 4

Skills & creativity.

Space for practical courses (e.g. lash or beauty taster sessions, book club start) that build confidence and open doors to income or hobbies.

Weeks 5–6

“Lay It Bare” & body confidence.

Therapeutic circles where women can share if they feel ready, letter-writing to people who have hurt them, healthy relationship brainstorming and movement-based confidence work.

Weeks 7–8

Safety & voice.

Self-defence classes, safety awareness (including safety apps and practical tools), plus spoken word workshops where women craft and rehearse their pieces.

Weeks 9–11

Integration & reflection.

Follow-up confidence sessions, performing poems within the group, affirmations, group feedback, and structured reflection exercises tracking growth since Week 1.

Week 12

Soft-life celebration.

End-of-cohort treat (such as a group meal or experience), final reflections, feedback forms and conversations about next steps, signposting and ongoing support.
