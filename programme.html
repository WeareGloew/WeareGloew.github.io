The G.L.O.E.W 12-week journey

A carefully designed programme blending healing, confidence, relationships, safety, money skills and soft-life moments — so growth isn’t just talked about, it’s lived.

Overview

Three phases of the cohort

Phase 1

Weeks 1–4: Safety, stories & self-awareness.

Welcome day, icebreakers, personality tests, story sharing, temperature checks, and reflective writing on life plans, trauma responses and identity.

Phase 2

Weeks 5–8: Confidence, body & voice.

Spoken word training, “Lay It Bare” therapeutic circles, movement workshops (such as pole art), self-defence and safety awareness.

Phase 3

Weeks 9–12: Future, freedom & soft life.

Money mindset, financial literacy, education and training opportunities, celebration sessions, reflection and an end-of-cohort treat.

Week by week

Sample cohort outline

Week 1

Welcome, story mapping & confidence check-in.

Icebreakers, personality tests, writing exercises on trauma responses, life goals, group agreements and pre-programme evaluation.

Week 2

Money, independence & imposter syndrome.

Mentoring around financial literacy, practical independence skills and mindset work around worthiness in work, education and money.

Week 3

Education, work & opportunity.

Support with CVs, training, referrals to courses, and upskilling pathways aligned with each young woman’s goals.

Week 4

Skills & creativity.

Practical tasters (beauty/lash basics, book club start) to build confidence and open new hobbies or income avenues.

Weeks 5–6

“Lay It Bare” & body confidence.

Therapeutic circles, letter-writing for closure, healthy relationship discussions, and movement-based confidence work.

Weeks 7–8

Safety & voice.

Self-defence sessions, practical safety education, spoken word workshops and confidence-building through expression.

Weeks 9–11

Integration & reflection.

Confidence boosters, poem performance, affirmations, feedback and structured reflection exercises charting growth.

Week 12

Soft-life celebration.

A group treat or experience, final reflections, feedback forms, signposting and personalised next-steps planning.
