About G.L.O.E.W

What is G.L.O.E.W?

Trauma-informed, but not trauma-defined.

G.L.O.E.W supports young women who have experienced harm and are ready to heal, grow, and rebuild safe, confident, soft lives.

Healing + skills + sisterhood.

We blend emotional healing, practical life skills, financial empowerment, creative workshops and community bonding.
