About G.L.O.E.W

What is G.L.O.E.W?

Our heart

Trauma-informed, but not trauma-defined.

G.L.O.E.W is a space for young women who have experienced or are at risk of domestic abuse, sexual violence, emotional harm, or unhealthy relationships — and are ready to move from survival to soft, sustainable growth.

Our approach

Healing + skills + sisterhood.

We blend therapeutic circles, creative expression, confidence building, practical life skills, and joyful experiences — so women don’t just talk about healing, they feel it in their bodies and daily lives.

The journey

Inside the 12-week G.L.O.E.W cohort

Weeks 1–4

Safety, stories & self-awareness.

Welcome circles, icebreakers, personality tools, and shared storytelling. Gentle introduction to boundaries, identity, and how trauma shows up in day-to-day life — without shame or judgement.

Weeks 5–8

Confidence, body & voice.

Confidence workshops, spoken word, movement-based sessions (like pole art), self-defence & safety, plus spaces to explore what healthy love really looks like.

Weeks 9–12

Future, freedom & soft life.

Money and independence, education and work opportunities, celebration of growth, and closing rituals that honour each woman’s journey and next steps.

Who G.L.O.E.W is for

G.L.O.E.W is designed for young women aged 18–25 who are ready to explore healing, boundaries, relationships, identity, and independence in a safe, non-judgemental space.

Referrals can come from professionals, organisations, or the young woman herself. Enrolment and consent forms can be completed online before the cohort begins.
